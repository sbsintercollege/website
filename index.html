Welcome to

SBS Inter College

SBS Inter College has been established by the prime grace & inspiration of lord Sh. Krishna. We have the vision to provide a modern learning level of education at Kheriya. We have the vision to provide a modern learning environment on the campus without damaging the roots of our rich culture and civilization.

SBS Inter College is a modern co-educational Hindi Medium School. established in 2010 by Shri Pratap Singh Ji, Shri Rishi Pal Singh Ji and Shri Veerpal Singh Ji. It provides a sounds academic, social, and moral education to all students, regardless of Cast, Creed & Religion.
